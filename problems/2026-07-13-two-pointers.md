# Problem: Container with most water.

**Category:** Two Pointers  
**Date:** 2026-07-13

## Problem Statement

Container with most water.

## Approach

Start with widest container. Move the shorter pointer inward — only way to possibly increase area.

## Complexity

- Time: O(n log n) in the general case
- Space: O(n) auxiliary

## Key Insight

Breaking the problem into smaller subproblems and recognising the pattern
is more valuable than memorising the solution.
